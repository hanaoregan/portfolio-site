Dear Humans: A Film Festival


These posters were created for a mock film festival, called Dear Humans, that I curated. Each film—Wall-E, I Am Legend, and Queen of the Sun—was chosen for the recurring theme of "good intentions gone wrong." I wanted to show the way that humans often attempt to do something good for the world, but that good intention often backfires on them.

The video is meant to compliment the posters and show more instances of "good intentions gone wrong."

The last three posters were previous versions of two of the final posters that, I think, still retain the right metaphor.

Poster size: 18x24


Medium: photography, InDesign, live-action video
