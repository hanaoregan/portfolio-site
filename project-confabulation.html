Confabulation Book


Before beginning the design of this book, I was told to pick a psychological disorder to research. I chose confabulation, the state of mind one enters when elderly or post-head trauma, which makes the affected person so confused that they begin to talk and live as though they live in another universe.

Therefore, my book design reflects the feeling of confabulation. The underlying structure remains throughout the book, representing reality, while the random outbursts of text act like the strange claims and actions of one affected by confabulation.


Medium: Adobe InDesign, hand-bound book
