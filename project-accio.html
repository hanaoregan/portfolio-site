Accio! App


I am a huge Harry Potter fan. Therefore it made sense for me to design an app for Harry Potter fans. This app is supposed to be for those Harry Potter fans, like myself, who grew up with the books. The app would be a place for fans to test their knowledge, duel other users, have a magical pet, have a wand, and be a part of a Hogwarts house.


Medium: Adobe InDesign
