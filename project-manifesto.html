Process of Invisibility


This book is all about showing the process, those layers that usually stay covered in a final outcome. I appreciate those many small decisions and thoughts that go in to a project, and used this book as a way to showcase those important details. Every decision, from leaving the binding openly-glued to showing the grid on certain pages, was made with the intent to draw other people's attention to the process of actually making a book.


Medium: 7x10" book

2016
